A break from social media

Hi, hello, and greetings to this silly little (quite shitty) website!

I (Valerie) made (well, it'll always be in development) this website with the goal of unplugging from the absolute slob that is social media. That shit wasn't very good for my mental health and shit, so I really needed to get out of the endless spiral of doom that is social media. And then I stumbled upon some random YouTube video talking about "the personal web" which really interested me, and I decided to give it a shot :3

I hadn't touched HTML and JavaScript in 4 years, so I've sorta just had to completely relearn both of those (really fucking stupid) coding languages. But I'd say I'm doing quite alright so far! I mean like, look at that slick side nav~ It changes the colour of the whole page and everything. I totally didn't copy paste it from w3school and then spend multiple hours changing the code to work for me, getting very frustrated in the process, to the point that I started barking because I go into puppy mode when I get frustrated AHEM!

So yeah! Thank you for checking this place out :3 I'll uploading a whole bunch of my thoughts here soon enough! And I'll probably end up uploading my hypno files here as well, cuz like, why not?

For now you can just go to the socials page through the side nav, and there I'll have all the links to all the places you can find me on. Feel free to send me a message (preferably on Discord) if you have any questions, feedback, or just wanna chat :3c
