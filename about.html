Hiya! My name is Valerie (she/her they/them), but online I've gone by "Stankendesokies" (or just "Stan" / "Stank") for years. I actually came up with the name because of Pokémon Go! I was like 11 or 12 years old back when that game came out, and I had to think of a good username for the first time in my life. A couple minutes before I had to pick a name in that game my mom had mentioned that I had stinky socks (but in Dutch, because I'm Dutch.) And so I decided to name myself "stinky socks" (but in Dutch,) however, due to my horrific dyslexia I spelled it like "stankendesokies". And like, that was kinda funny, and it's an extremely unique name. So from that moment on I always called myself "Stankendesokies" in any game and social media.

Anyway so, I'm an 18 year old trans woman living in the so called Netherlands. And this is my personal website, where I will share my thoughts and feelings and whatever random bullshit comes to mind :3

I'm an anarchist, and that'll definitely be noticable throughout this website. If you think that's annoying, or whatever. ¯\_ (ツ)_/¯ I don't really care?? It's my website I'll write whatever the fuck I want.
